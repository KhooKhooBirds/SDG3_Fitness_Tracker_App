# Project Summary: TRACKDEEZSTATS

## 1. Project Overview & SDG Alignment
**Target SDG:** Goal 3 - Good Health and Well-Being
**Product Vision:** TrackDeezStats is a game-based fitness and health-tracking application developed to motivate users to adopt and sustain a more active lifestyle. In practice, many individuals struggle to maintain consistent exercise and hydration routines. To address this challenge, the prototype integrates comprehensive activity tracking with a reward-based mechanism, encouraging users to gradually build healthier daily habits.

## 2. Core Features Developed in Prototype
Based on our SDLC planning and design phases, the final prototype includes the following core functionalities:
* **Dashboard & Activity Rings:** Realtime visualization of daily movements, exercise minutes, and standing hours.
* **Comprehensive Tracking:** Dedicated modules for Step Tracking, Distance Travelled, and a Workout Log supporting both quick selection and manual entry.
* **Hydration Monitor:** A quickadd interface to track daily water intake against personal goals.
* **Gamification (Quest Board):** Daily and weekly challenges and long-term milestones to keep users engaged.
* **Rewards System:** An integrated store where users can redeem earned points for digital vouchers (e.g., Shopee, Lazada, MIXUE).
* **Progress Analytics:** Visual graphs providing insights into weekly and monthly health performance trends.

## 3. SDLC Results & Evaluation Summary
*This section summarizes our findings from the testing and evaluation phase:*

* **Iterative Improvement:** The prototype evolved from initial low-fidelity wireframes in Sprint 1 to this final version in Sprint 2, focusing heavily on UI/UX enhancements.
* **User Feedback & Adjustments:** * During our peer evaluation, testers found the gamification aspect highly motivating. Based on feedback regarding ease of use, we implemented the "Quick Add" buttons in the Hydration Tracker for faster logging.
* **Final Outcome:** The prototype successfully demonstrates a clear, user-friendly flow that aligns perfectly with our initial Product Vision and Agile Sprint goals.
