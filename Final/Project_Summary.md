# Project Summary: TRACKDEEZSTATS

## 1. Project Overview & SDG Alignment
**Target:** SDG3 - Good Health and Well-Being

**Product Vision:** TRACKDEEZSTATS is a gamified health-tracking app. It motivates users to maintain an active lifestyle. Many individuals struggle with consistent exercise and hydration. Our prototype solves this by combining activity tracking with a reward system to build healthier habits.

## 2. Core Features Developed in Prototype
Based on our design phases, the final prototype includes these core functionalities:
* **Dashboard & Activity Rings:** The interface displays daily movements, exercise minutes, and standing hours in real time.
* **Tracking system:** Users can log their steps, distance, and specific workouts manually or via quick presets.
* **Hydration Board:** A tool allows users to quickly track their daily water intake.
* **Quest Board:** Daily and weekly challenges consistently motivate users to reach long-term health milestones.
* **Rewards System:** Users can exchange their earned points for digital vouchers from popular brands.
* **Progress Analytics:** Interactive graphs provide clear insights into weekly and monthly health trends.

## 3. SDLC Results & Evaluation Summary
*Testing and evaluation showed these main results:*

* **Iterative Improvement:** Sprint 2 optimized our initial wireframes with a strong focus on UI/UX enhancements.
* **User Feedback & Adjustments:** Peer feedback validated our gamification and inspired "Quick Add" buttons for faster hydration logging.
* **Final Outcome:** The final prototype delivers a user-friendly flow that meets our initial vision and Agile goals.
