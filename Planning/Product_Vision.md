# Product Vision - TRACKDEEZSTATS

## 1. Problem Statement
For our target group, keeping to regular excrcise is large challenge. A survery of 38 respondents showed that mood(57.9%), stress(55.3%) and time(47.3%)were the biggest obstacles. Nearly 70% of users want to enter fitness data for no more than 3 minutes. These finding show that the market has an urgent need for efficient motion tracking solution.

## 2. Target Users (Stakeholders)
- **Active Users:** Young adults under the age of 25 represent 81.6% of our active user base.
- **User Profile:** Our primary user includes university students who exercise regularly.

## 3. Proposed Solution
**TRACKDEEZSTATS** provides a gamified health platform aimed at solving these specific challenges:
- **Efficient Tracking:** The platform includes MVP features with high user demand, such as step tracking and progress analysis.
- **Gamified Motivation:** A reward system improves exercise motivation for 65.7% of our respondents.
- **Incentivized Engagement:** We provide discount vouchers and digital milestones to encourage develop long-term healthy habits.

## 4. Alignment with SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being)
This project aligns with SDG 3 to promote healthy lives and well-being for all ages.

The functions provided by TRACKDEEZSTATS are designed to helpuser overcome psychological and time fitness. For example, functions such as drinking water reminder and continuous tracking encourage college students to develop a healthy lifestyle.

## 5. Value Proposition
TRACKDEEZSTATS allows users to adjust their workout time and plan according to their personal schedules. The application provides a short recording process to save users time and avoid tedious experience. We convert daily health data into rewards, so that personal health is within reach.
