# Product Vision - TRACKDEEZSTATS

## 1. Problem Statement
Maintaining consistent exercise is a significant challenge for our target group. Research with 38 respondents shows that mood (57.9%), stress (55.3%), and time (47.4%) are the biggest barriers. Nearly 70% of users want to spend less than 3 minutes inputting their fitness data. These findings show a clear need for an efficient tracking solution.

## 2. Target Users (Stakeholders)
- **Active Users:** Young adults under the age of 25 represent 81.6% of our active user base.
- **User Profile:** Our primary audience includes university students who exercise casually.

## 3. Proposed Solution
**TRACKDEEZSTATS** provides a gamified health platform to address these specific challenges:
- **Efficient Tracking:** The platform includes step tracking and progress analytics as high-demand MVP features.
- **Gamified Motivation:** A reward system improves exercise motivation for 65.7% of our respondents.
- **Incentivized Engagement:** We offer discount vouchers and digital milestones to encourage long-term healthy habits.

## 4. Alignment with SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-Being)
This project aligns with SDG 3 to promote healthy lives and well-being for all ages.

TRACKDEEZSTATS provides functional requirements to help users overcome psychological and time related fitness barriers. Features like water intake reminders and continuous tracking encourage a healthy lifestyle for university students. 

## 5. Value Proposition
TRACKDEEZSTATS allows users to adjust workout time and modify based on their personal schedules. The application provides a short logging process to save users time and prevent a tedious experience. We convert daily health data into availability rewards to make personal well-being more accessible.
