* Product Backlog Written as Scenarios

** Must Have
1. As a user, I expect a short fitness logging process that feels intuitive for ease of use.
2. As a user, I prefer continuous metric tracking readily available on the home page to encourage me.
3. As a student, I prefer tangible rewards compared to digital rewards because it’s more gratifying.
4. As a regular user, I want a simple interface for easy use.

** Should Have
1. As a university student, I would prefer the tracking metrics for food/diet or general progress.
2. As an amateur gym goer, I would prefer personalized workouts to general workout challenges.
3. As a busy student, daily fitness quests should be short and flexible to accommodate my school schedule.

** Could Have
1. As an amateur gym-goer, I want to be paired with similar users to encourage my participation and growth.
2. As a user, I would find a leaderboard motivational and encourage my engagement with the application.

** Won't Have
1. As a user, I would rather use guest mode to prevent long login process.
